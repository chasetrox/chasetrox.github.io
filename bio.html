ABOUT ME

Chase Troxell grew up in Weston, CT and graduated from Weston High School in 2014. At Weston High School, Chase was the president of the school theatre company and was active in his community through NHS and the Boy Scouts. He is currently enrolled in Tufts University's School of Arts and Sciences where he studies Computer Science and is a member of JumboCode and a founding member of his fraternity, Pi Delta.
